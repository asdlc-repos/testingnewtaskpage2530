# Overview

An internal application for WSO2 employees to request time off and for managers to review those requests.

# Personas

- Employee — requests leave and checks their balance
- Manager — approves or rejects leave requests from their team

# Features

- An employee requests time off by selecting dates and a reason.
- Managers see pending requests from people who report to them.
- A manager approves or rejects each request.
- Employees see the status of their submitted requests.
- Everyone can view how many leave days they have remaining.
